Future Perfect Tense

The future perfect tense is used to talk about actions that will be completed before a specific time in the future.

Sentence structure

Subject + will have + past participle of the main verb + other.

Examples

  • I'm sure he will have finished his book by next month.
  • I will have finished my homework before you arrive.
  • She will have graduated from college by next year.
  • They will have had their lunch by now.

Negative

will not have + past participle of the main verb

Examples

  • I will not have finished my homework by tomorrow morning.
  • She will not have graduated from college by next summer.
  • They will not have had their lunch by the time you get there.

Questions

Will + subject + have + past participle of the main verb?

Examples

  • Will you have finished your homework by the time I arrive?
  • Will she have graduated from college by next year?
  • Will they have had their lunch by now?
